The growing database of Bone2Gene

Current Bone2Gene Database

Bone2Gene is under development and so far has around 2000 X-rays from over 1000 individuals with 28 different bone disorders, and these numbers are growing.

Get in touch

Bone2Gene currently has collaborators from 8 different University Hospitals in Germany, as well as different medical centers in the United States, South Africa, Georgia, Slovenia, and Australia. We are actviely working on expanding this network and we very much welcome new collabrators.
